about me!

Hi! I am an 18 year old photographer, art appreciator, obsessive organizer, math nerd, and full-time student currently located in Greensboro, North Carolina. My interests are all over the place, and my goal with this website is to have a place to dump the work that I do and the things that interest me.

Photography has been my major artistic medium since 2013. The ability of a camera to capture moments, and the ability of the photographer to capture and create feelings and moods has always intrigued me. Photography has always been a perfect blend between technical and creative skills for me. I have explored digital photography and Photoshop, as well as film photography and silver-gelatin printing. Most of my photographic work is surreal, as my fascination has always been with representing the world in unique and unusual ways.

My other interests lie in science, math, physics, and computer science, and I have been exploring these disciplines in many ways. I have taken physics courses for two years, learning everything from electromagnetism to general relativity. I have taken calculus courses and applied my knowledge in national mathematical modeling competitions. I have implemented computer programming as a tool throughout many parts of my life, and explored the wonderful possibilities that computers yield. These are all incredibly interesting to me, and I hope to continue to explore these disciplines.

I hope that this website will help me to develop my wide variety of interests and understand where they all intersect, as well as help others understand what my passions are and how I interact with the world.
