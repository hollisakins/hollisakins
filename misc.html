projects // miscellaneous



For my final project for an observational astronomy class at Guilford College, I used a method called Lucky Imaging to photograph planets and incoorporate them into artistic photographs. The project was exhibited as a poster presentation at the Guilford Undergraduate Symposium, and turned into an article which was published in the October 2018 edition of The Physics Teacher.
